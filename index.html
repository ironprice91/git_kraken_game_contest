:video_game: How to play

There are bugs in your code! Use the arrow keys ( :arrow_left: :arrow_right: :arrow_up: :arrow_down: ) to dodge the bugs!

Collect the git commands for 5 points a piece:

Collect cherries:cherries: and drop them as bombs by pressing spacebar.

Use the d key to detonate :bomb: and get rid of the bugs (10 points a piece).

** On Chrome and using the Vimium plugin? Press Shift + D to detonate. Phaser Issue
